My name is Edward Tang and I am a junior at Dublin High School. Currently I consider myself a jack of all trades and have yet to decide what I will master. I am interested in STEM and have aspirations to attend a university with a strong department in that field. However, I am going to keep my options open for as long as possible before I decide. In school I am taking a wide variety of courses. I enjoy challenging myself, so I am determined to take the most heavy workload possible.

Balancing multiple AP classes with extracurriculars requires a great deal of time-management skills. My schedule constantly needs to be tweaked so I can study for an upcoming test or work on a group project.

White Stag Leadership Camp is a a summer camp that teaches leadership skills to youth. After completing the program as a candidate, I joined Youth Staff and became a counselor. Part of my commitment is to attend monthly weekend-long meetings. Although this leaves me with very little time to focus on academics, my leadership skills have grown immensely. I am currently in my third year as a member of Youth Staff. I plan to continue with the program through the rest of high school.
