My name is Edward Tang and I am a junior at Dublin High School. Most of the time I am busy doing homework and keeping up with my classes. I haven't yet decided what I want to study in college, so right now I'm taking a wide variety of subjects. On the occasions that I find myself with nothing to study for, I like to spend my time reading books. My parents want me to read about chemistry and calculus, but I much prefer the fantasy genre. Among my favorites are the Redwall series and the Inheritance cycle. For excersize, I enjoy biking and hiking. Some days I ride my bike to the library after school for some peace and quiet. I like to hike local mountains and trails during breaks or long weekends. As member of a venture crew, I also get the opportunity to camp out every month for a whole weekend. It's a welcome respite from the torments of my older brother and younger sister. With parental attention split between the three of us, I am often left to my own devices. I have a great deal of experience with procrastination as a result. I have yet to master the art of self-motivation, but the timely creation of this website is certainly a testament to my progress.
